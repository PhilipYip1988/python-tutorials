## LateX Text (MathJax)

|---|---|---|

|normal text|```$\text{x}$```|$\text{x}$|

|math text with tilde|```$\tilde{x}$```|$\tilde{x}$|
|math text with wide tilde|```$\widetilde{x}$```|$\widetilde{x}$|
